Welcome to COMP 1950

COMP 1950 - Web Design & Development 2 is a part-time course offered by the School of Computing And Academic Studies. This course is focused on equipping students with tools that apply directly to professional web development. In this 12 week long course, you will not only extend your knowledge on HTML & CSS, but also learn to make responsive web pages, SASS, grid, git/gitHub.

Why COMP 1950 ?

COMP 1950 follows on from COMP 1850 Applied Web Development Level 1. This is a hands-on course and students who already understand HTML and CSS will explore web development in detail with new topics.
